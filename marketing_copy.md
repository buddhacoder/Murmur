# Murmur Marketing Copy & Positioning

## The Core Thesis
**Typing is the bottleneck to human thought.**
When you type, your brain is forced to slow down to match the speed of your fingers. You filter, you hesitate, and you lose the nuance of your complex ideas. Murmur is the bridge that lets you work at the speed of thought.

## Taglines & Hooks
* "Never type again."
* "The greatest prompting framework in the world isn't an acronym. It's your voice."
* "Verbal prompting is the foundation of AI mastery. Before you learn any other framework, stop typing your thoughts and start speaking them."
* "The ultimate clinical copilot doesn't wait for your fingers to catch up."

## The "Secret" to AI Mastery
People spend hours trying to learn complex "Prompt Engineering" frameworks (like STAR, RTF, etc.). 
The actual secret? **Verbal Prompting.** 
When you speak naturally to an AI, you automatically provide 100x more context, nuance, and background information than you ever would if you were exhausted by typing it all out. Murmur unlocks the highest level of AI interaction by removing the friction between your brain and the text box.

## Security & Privacy Angle
"Why pay $1,500/year for software that uploads your patient data to the cloud? Murmur is 100% local, zero-latency, and lives permanently on your hard drive."
