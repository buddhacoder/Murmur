The fastest, most private way to dictate on Windows.

As a board-certified anesthesiologist and developer, I spend countless hours typing detailed clinical notes and code. I built Murmur because I needed a blazing-fast, highly accurate voice-to-text app that typed everywhere I placed my cursor—without uploading my private recordings to the cloud.

Most dictation software is sluggish, incorrectly types words, or forces you to send your personal data to remote servers. Murmur fixes this by instantly typing what you say right on your machine.

Why Murmur is better:

  • Lightning Fast: Zero latency. Your words appear exactly where your cursor is.
  • 100% Local & Private: Audio never leaves your computer. Powered entirely by local AI models.
  • No Subscriptions: Just an honest, standalone tool you download and run without arbitrary limits.

After launching on macOS, a native Windows version became our most requested feature. I'm incredibly excited to finally get it into your hands.

Download for Windows (.exe)

Compatible with Windows 10 & 11 (64-bit). No installation required.

🕹️ How to Use Murmur:

  1. Download & Open: Download the .exe file and double-click to run it (a purple icon will appear in your system tray).
  2. Click Anywhere: Place your cursor inside any text box, EHR, Word document, or browser window.
  3. Hold & Speak: Press and hold the Right-Alt key on your keyboard. Speak, release the key, and your text will instantly appear!
